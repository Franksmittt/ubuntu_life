We are a representation and distribution partner for approved international suppliers, not a factory. Ubuntu introduces named product lines (for example SANI-99™ for AGRI, SANI-AMANZI, Tonno Bonno), supports RFQs and programmes, and coordinates compliant supply into Southern Africa.

Start with a technical and regulatory fit conversation. We map channel partners, pilot pathways, and documentation expectations, then act as your in-market sales and programme interface, so your team is not guessing from abroad.

Agricultural biosecurity (animal health and crop-care positioning where registered), safe water (household, municipal, and emergency treatment), and food security (high-protein shelf-stable nutrition), plus broader institutional supply where audited representation is required.

Models are agreed per brand and programme: representation, distributor-style supply, project-based procurement support, or blended structures. Fees, margins, and responsibilities are set up-front so both sides know who owns sales, stock, and service.
