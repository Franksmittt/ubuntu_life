Shelf-stable nutrition

High-protein, long-life foods for retail, feeding schemes, and institutional kitchens where logistics resilience and programme documentation matter as much as price.

Home Core pillars Shelf-stable nutrition
This pillar

Protein that holds up on the shelf and in tenders

We represent brands with manufacturing discipline and export readiness, and we align packaging, shelf-life claims, and paperwork with what regional buyers and programme offices actually audit.

Whether you are a retailer building an affordable protein aisle or a programme office planning multi-month coverage, we brief supply in structured volumes, not opportunistic spot loads.

Canned marine protein for the South African trade is expected to meet NRCS VC 8014 style controls (thermal process integrity, hermetic seals, heavy-metal limits). We help buyers and programme sponsors interpret what that means for pallets landing in Johannesburg, Durban, or regional DCs, so receiving inspections and grant audits tell one story.

Tonno Bonno International is our anchor range in this pillar: tuna chunks and shredded lines, pilchards, and sardines in brine, oil, and sauce formats suited to retail shelves and bulk feeding programmes. Open the Tonno Bonno overview and individual SKU pages when you need case counts, oil-versus-brine trade-offs, and copy blocks nutrition sign-off can reuse.

Programme offices often need bilingual sleeve notes, grant narrative language, and evidence of named-brand continuity across seasons; retailers need planograms, promotional windows, and DC-friendly mixed pallets. Ubuntu keeps technical, procurement, and marketing stakeholders on the same thread so nobody is working from an outdated attachment.
