Protect Your Livestock from Disease Outbreaks with Proven Biosecurity

Veterinary-grade disinfection solutions designed to eliminate pathogens, control outbreaks, and protect agricultural productivity.

Home Products SANI-99™ for AGRI
The problem

Disease spreads fast across farms and equipment

Foot and Mouth Disease (FMD) and other infectious diseases spread rapidly through farms, transport systems, and equipment - causing severe economic loss and operational disruption.

The solution: SANI-99™ for AGRI is a high-performance, veterinary-grade disinfectant delivering up to 99.99995% pathogen elimination (Log 7 efficacy).

It provides full-spectrum virucidal, bactericidal, and fungicidal action, long-lasting protection (up to 90 days residual effect), and proven effectiveness against FMD, Avian Influenza, E. coli, and Salmonella.

Application

Suitable across farm operations

Farms & feedlots

Routine disinfection for high-risk agricultural environments.

Veterinary clinics & transport vehicles

Control contamination from movement and treatment workflows.

Abattoirs, equipment & facilities

Protect production zones and critical hygiene touchpoints.

Efficacy framing

Pathogen coverage you can brief

Laboratory and field dossiers from the manufacturer reference high log-reduction performance against a defined pathogen panel and soils loadings. Request the latest independent test summaries and SANS-aligned certificates when you build a tender pack.

Bacterial & foodborne

E. coli, Salmonella (including Typhimurium), Listeria monocytogenes, Staphylococcus aureus, Enterococcus hirae, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, and Greasy Pig / dysentery-type complexes referenced in manufacturer technical marketing.

Viral & notifiable

Avian Influenza, Newcastle Disease, Paramyxovirus, Foot and Mouth Disease, PRRS, swine influenza family agents, and pox-type viruses listed in manufacturer pathogen tables for veterinary hygiene use classes.

Operator & stock comfort

Marketing materials highlight reduced fragrance load versus legacy disinfectants, no secondary rinse requirement on many surfaces when label allows, and compatibility with foam lances, low-pressure sprayers, and manual pump packs.

Dilution & coverage

One reference batch: 96 g in 16 L

The manufacturer publishes a baseline mix of 96 g powder dissolved in 16 litres of potable water, then scales surface coverage by contamination tier. Use your official dilution card for audited sites; the table below is a conversational summary only.

Soil / risk level Powder Water Indicative coverage Typical contexts
High 96 g 16 L ~160 m² Outbreak zones, heavily soiled abattoir floors, deep litter turnaround.
Medium 96 g 16 L ~400 m² Routine poultry and pig housing, parlours, active feed lines.
Low 96 g 16 L ~800 m² Equipment yards, trailers, admin entry mats, preventative perimeter work.

Figures mirror manufacturer brochure guidance. Independent agronomists or state vets may prescribe different application rates during declared disease events.

Compliance & credentials

What procurement teams ask for

Ubuntu can bundle the evidence pack your technical desk needs. Availability of specific certificates should be confirmed at enquiry because formulations and registrations evolve.

Standards & testing

Manufacturer dossiers cite EN 1276, EN 13697, EN 1656-style suspension testing, and South African SANS bactericidal / fungicidal protocols with high organic challenge simulations (albumin, hard water).

Foot & Mouth context

UK DEFRA lists include approved dilution pathways for statutory FMD control; SANI-99 family materials appear in that disinfectant schedule for defined use cases.

South African registration

NRCS chemical disinfectant registration (VC 8054 family) and Halal / Proudly South African branding are referenced in manufacturer collateral for VC-listed supply.

Biocidal product types

EU BPR positioning includes PT3 (veterinary hygiene) and PT4 (food and feed area) claims where applicable to the SKU you import.

Shelf & storage

Unopened powder is typically specified for multi-year stability stored cool, dry, and out of direct sun; mixed solution life is shorter and label-dependent.

PPE profile

Compared with formaldehyde fumigation or chlorine gas systems, manufacturer safety sheets promote lighter routine PPE for standard spraying tasks, subject to SDS review.
