Clean Drinking Water Anywhere - Fast, Scalable, Proven

Transform contaminated water into safe drinking water in under 30 minutes.

Home Products SANI-AMANZI
The problem

Unsafe water creates health and economic risk

Over 2.2 billion people globally lack access to safe drinking water, leading to disease, economic loss, and reduced quality of life.

The solution: SANI AMANZI™ is a point-of-use water purification solution designed for governments, NGOs, rural communities, farms, and livestock operations.

Performance includes 99.99% pathogen removal, elimination of E. coli, Salmonella, and Cholera, and alignment with SANS drinking water standards even in highly contaminated environments.

How it works

Simple four-step field process

Add 6g sachet to 20L water

Start with measured dosage for consistent treatment.

Stir and activate

Ensure complete activation throughout the water volume.

Allow 30 minutes

Wait for treatment cycle completion before consumption.

Field protocol

Five steps every operator follows

  1. Collect 20 L of raw water in a clean vessel.
  2. Dose one sealed 6 g sachet (or bulk equivalent from your dosing chart).
  3. Agitate vigorously for 1–2 minutes to dissolve actives and start flocculation.
  4. Rest 30 minutes undisturbed for kill kinetics and settling.
  5. Decant / filter the supernatant through a clean cloth into a storage container; never drink the sludge layer that concentrates metals and debris.
Microbiology

Targets cited in lab packs

SANAS-recognised laboratory work quoted by the manufacturer demonstrates aggressive reduction of faecal coliforms, including Vibrio cholerae, Salmonella spp., Shigella spp., and generic E. coli down to near-zero counts per 100 mL when the dwell time is respected.

Developers also highlight retained performance against selected antibiotic-resistant phenotypes, relevant where superbugs move through informal water networks.
