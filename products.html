1. Agricultural biosecurity

Veterinary-grade disinfection

  • SANI-99™ for AGRI

    Powder-activated veterinary disinfectant positioned for poultry, pigs, abattoirs, and perimeter hygiene. Manufacturer dossiers reference broad bacterial and viral panels (including Avian Influenza, FMD, and E. coli), high log-reduction data under soiled conditions, and DEFRA-listed use classes where applicable. Supplied in bulk tubs (1 kg, 5 kg) and concentrated sachet boxes for sprayers and foot baths.

    Typical deployment covers house entry foot baths, vehicle wheel sprays, loading bays, staff hygiene points, and corridor fogging where programmes require repeatable dilution and contact time. Powder stock is easier to warehouse than drums of liquid disinfectant, with lower shipping weight per active dose and fewer empty-container disposal issues on remote sites.

    Ubuntu Life Resources supports distributors and end users with import documentation, batch traceability expectations, and routing into Southern African channels. The full product page includes dilution guidance, pathogen groupings, and compliance notes so technical and procurement teams can align internal SOPs before rollout.

    • Bulk tubs for high-throughput sprayers and continuous foot-bath top-up.
    • Sachet formats for accurate small-batch mixing on farm and in depots.
    • Link to pillar narrative for sector strategy and programme framing.

2. Water purification

Point-of-use sanitizing powder

  • SANI-AMANZI

    Chlorine-free powder that oxidises waterborne pathogens (including cholera and typhoid-class agents) while flocculating arsenic, iron, and fluoride into settleable sludge. Field protocol: dose, agitate, 30-minute dwell, decant through cloth. 6 g sachets (20 L each), 5-pack retail sleeves (~100 L), and 1 kg bulk bags (~3 000 L) for municipalities and NGOs.

    The same chemistry suits household jars, school drums, clinic buckets, and community tanks where turbid surface water or compromised boreholes would overload chlorine-only approaches. Carrying sachets or measured bulk into settlements avoids repeated bottled-water logistics and gives a documented dwell-and-decant rhythm trainers can demonstrate in one visit.

    Programme partners typically pair SANI-AMANZI with hygiene messaging (clean storage vessels, cloth filters, hand washing) so treated water is not re-contaminated after decanting. Ubuntu Life Resources can align pallet volumes, bilingual sleeve copy where required, and enquiry paths for WASH tenders or emergency pre-positioning stock.

    • Single-sachet clarity: one 6 g unit treats about 20 L when instructions are followed.
    • Bulk bags for councils, NGOs, and district health staging warehouses.
    • Full product page: five-step field protocol, metals behaviour, and quality credentials.
